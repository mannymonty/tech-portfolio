# FinAI Adoption - AI Enablement Video Series

## One-Liner
A series of explainer videos designed to drive adoption of AI tools across the Finance organization, teaching non-technical finance professionals how to apply AI to real business workflows.

## The Problem
Early in the AI tool rollout within Finance, adoption was low. The tools existed, but finance professionals didn't know how to apply them to their actual work. Generic training materials weren't landing. People needed to see AI solving *their* specific problems, in *their* context.

## What I Did
I produced a series of explainer videos targeting specific finance use cases, not generic "here's what AI can do" content, but practical walkthroughs showing how to use AI tools for real workflows that finance teams encounter daily. Each video was designed to:

- Start with a recognizable pain point ("You spend 2 hours formatting this report every week")
- Show the AI tool solving that exact problem, step by step
- End with a clear call to action and next steps

This was evangelism work: identifying the adoption gap, creating the content to close it, and distributing it through internal channels.
